I am a doctoral researcher in the Computational Aerosciences Lab at the University of Michigan, advised by Prof. Karthik Duraisamy. My research focuses on reliable and efficient scientific machine learning, spanning probabilistic operator learning for uncertainty-aware modeling of PDE-governed systems and numerical stability analysis of low- and mixed-precision computations. I am interested in developing numerically stable, data-efficient, and computationally efficient methods for scientific machine learning, including mixed-precision strategies for reliable scientific computing and probabilistic operators with scalable inference.
